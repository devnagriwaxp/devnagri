CLAN REWARD

The Clan Reward system is a reward system in the Devanagri project. It is based on various factors such as the number of active and inactive players in the game, as well as the number of members in the clan. The Clan Reward system is also influenced by the tax generated from the clan's land plots. The primary purpose of this reward system is to enhance the project and educate people about it. Clan leaders play a crucial role in teaching others about the project and guiding them to become skilled players, thus fostering a sense of community.
