Clan system is an important part of Devnagari project. Devnagri land map have four sides and all different sides show different clan area. Four clans can be rule in each side of map. All clan have their own ability to set taxes on land plots and things which produce anything on their respective clan lands. All tax is stored in a clan pool and it can be used for clan area development, reward for clan members and increase the new people and exchange of plots.


How to get a clan?

To get a clan, first step is that a player need to buy a land plot and a clan banner. This clan banner has a clan name on it and itâ€™s placed on one land plot in any sides of map. To choose a side of map for your clan, player need to collect project players cards. Highest card holder can choose a land side first from the available sides A,B,C & D.


What is player cards?

Player card is a part of clan system. Each player of Devnagri project can get their card by asking in the project group. First card is given to the player who is asking for the same. Ten cards of each player is minted. Player need to share the character name along with the picture and story if possible. Other remaining nine cards can come in the market for sell. Each card is counted as a vote for the clan system.
